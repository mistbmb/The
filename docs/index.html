The Studio

Pus&Co is an Istanbul-based architectural studio led by Bora Pus. We specialize in bridging the gap between complex technical requirements and human-centric design.

With over 14 years of experience, we have delivered projects in Turkey, Azerbaijan, Turkmenistan, Haiti, and Georgia. Our work spans from massive infrastructure hubs to intimate private residences.

Our Approach

We believe great architecture emerges from collaboration. Our process integrates client engagement, research-led design, and technical precision through BIM workflows.
